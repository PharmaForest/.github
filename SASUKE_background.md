## (Japanese version) SASUKE（サスケ）：

クナイ、手裏剣などあらゆる忍び道具を操り、変身変化もお手の物。神出鬼没の忍び（甲賀者）。伊賀との骨肉を争う戦いに身を投じるものの、伊賀忍術も使いこなし、甲賀や伊賀という構造を壊すことを目的としている節も垣間見える。一見すると、甲賀の身でありながら伊賀忍術も扱う矛盾から、既存の枠に対する破壊的な思想の持主ともとれるが、その先に新しい地平線を見ているためのまずは破壊、すなわち最終的には創造的な思想の持主ともとれる。得意技はメドローアと褒め殺し、口癖は「はじけてまざれっ」。現世においては、SASとRのそれぞれの高等忍術を造作もなく操るプログラマ忍者として存在している。ひたすら人を肯定しほめちぎる。思想好き。

## (English version) SASUKE :

A shinobi (of the Kōga clan) who masterfully wields every kind of ninja tool—kunai, shuriken, and more—and is also adept at transformation techniques. Elusive and unpredictable, he throws himself into a bitter, blood-soaked conflict with the Iga clan. Yet, he also commands Iga ninjutsu, hinting at a deeper aim: to dismantle the very structure of both Kōga and Iga.

At first glance, this contradiction—being of Kōga while wielding Iga techniques—suggests a destructive mindset that rejects existing frameworks. However, it can also be seen as a creative philosophy: destruction as a necessary first step toward envisioning and building a new horizon.

His signature moves include “Medroa” and the art of “killing with praise.” His catchphrase is: “Burst and merge!”.

In the modern world, he exists as a programmer-ninja who effortlessly commands advanced “ninjutsu” in both SAS and R. Above all, he relentlessly affirms and praises others, showering them with positivity. A lover of ideas and philosophy.
